
# Store Media

Photography and other media related to a first-party Cookies store.

## Structure

`StoreMedia`

## Fields

| Name | Type | Tags | Description |
|  --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `facade` | [`Array<MediaItem>`](/doc/models/media-item.md) | Required | Facade photography of a Cookies store. |
| `inside` | [`Array<MediaItem>`](/doc/models/media-item.md) | Required | Inside photography of a Cookies store. |
| `masthead` | [`Array<MediaItem>`](/doc/models/media-item.md) | Required | Masthead (featured) photography for a Cookies store. |
